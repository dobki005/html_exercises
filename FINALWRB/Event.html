Field Trips

Do you have a group of children or adults (or both) that would like to visit Wild Rumpus? We would love to have you! Please call us (612-920-5005) and reserve a date and time so that we can tell the animals we're having company! We would be happy to give a short tour of the store, introduce some of the animals, and read a story to your group.














Local Authors

Wild Rumpus is excited to bring authors from around the country (and the world!) to local schools, media centers, and classrooms. We offer pre-sales of books to students and teachers. Authors are happy to sign and personalize books at the visit.
